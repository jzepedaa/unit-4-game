Soccer Ball Number Collector!

    You will be given a random number at the start of the game

    There are four soccer balls below. By clicking on a ball you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

    You win the game by matching your total score to the random number (Number To Get), you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

    The value of each ball is hidden form you until you click on it

    Each time the game starts, the values of each ball will change
